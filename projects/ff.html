Family Fisticuffs

❮ ❯

Date

October 2022

Description

Family Fisticuffs is a 2D Arcade game all about getting the high score! When your family members bump into eachother, they start arguing! Break them apart and keep peace! The other programmers on the team helped with porting the game to PS Vita.

My Role

I programmed all the main gameplay mechanics and lead the team through their first game jam. All gameplay systems were coded by me. This includes the high score serialization, scoring system, player controller, and family member logic. This game won LoJam 2022 Industry Judges' First Pick.
